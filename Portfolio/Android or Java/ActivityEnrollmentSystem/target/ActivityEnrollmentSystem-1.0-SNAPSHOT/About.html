Activity Groups

Pisoc Society

Dive into Code with Pisoc! Calling all computer enthusiasts! Whether you're a coding whiz or just starting your digital journey, Pisoc, the University of Bradford CS Society, is your hub for all things tech. Level Up & Explore: Sharpen your skills with workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects. Dive into diverse fields like web dev, AI, and cybersecurity. Network & Learn: Connect with like-minded peers, potential mentors, and future employers. Share your knowledge and learn from experienced members. Fun with Code: Coding challenges, game jams, and inspiring guest lectures keep learning exciting! Open to All: No prior experience needed, just bring your passion!

Swimming Society

Make Waves and Friends with the [University Name] Swimming Society! Calling all water lovers! Are you a seasoned swimmer looking to refine your strokes, or a curious beginner just dipping your toes in? The Swimming Society welcomes everyone to dive into the world of aquatic fun and fitness. Sharpen Your Skills: Our coached sessions cater to all levels. Whether you're mastering the butterfly or perfecting your front crawl, we'll help you refine your technique, build endurance, and achieve your swimming goals. Find Your Fin Family: Join a supportive community of fellow aquatic enthusiasts! Bond with teammates over shared passion for the water, celebrate personal bests (and cheer each other on!), and build lasting friendships that extend beyond the pool. More Than Just Laps: We offer a variety of activities, from casual morning swims to friendly competitions. We also host social events, ensuring there's something for everyone, both in and out of the water. Ready to Make a Splash? Join the Swimming Society and discover the joy of swimming in a supportive and friendly environment.
